Associate Professor, Ph.D.

I am currently a professor and the Vice Dean with the School of Computer Science and Technology, Shanghai University of Electric Power. I received the B.S. degree in information security and Ph.D. degree in computer science and technology from Hunan University, Changsha, China, in 2007 and 2014, respectively. My research interests include applied cryptography, network security, security protocol analysis, trustworthy artificial intelligence, artificial intelligence for smart grids, privacy preserving, and cloud computing security.

I am served as a TPC member or a chairman for ICC, PST, ICPDS, and acted as a reviewer for IEEE transaction on Communications，IEEE Internet of Things Journal, INFOCOM, ICC, Security and Communication Networks, Peer-to-Peer Networking and Applications, etc.
